What Is Our Paper About?

Background of the Study

Music has been a part of human history and tradition since the ancient times and is still a dominant factor in human living to this day. Music affects our mood. Depending on the genre. “Happy” or upbeat music invokes in us happy emotions, while “sad” or mellow music invokes within us sad emotions (Ahmad & Rana, 2015).

Though focus is indeed needed by the reader to comprehend what he/she is reading, in some cases however, readers do not fully appreciate a passage since they get bored in the middle of reading. Some view reading as a menial task and would like to minimize the amount of reading if possible. Thus, to resolve the issue of boredom while reading, individuals tend to listen to their favorite kind of music while doing this task.

Knowing this, the researchers would like to know if the genre is significant when one reads a passage. The researchers also aim to see what genre proves to be more effective while one is reading, whether it be classical music or pop music.

Statement of the Problem

General: Is there any significant difference when comparing classical and pop music when measuring its effect on the reading comprehension of the students?

Specific: Do the students tend to score better in tests involving reading when classical music is being played in the background? Do the students tend to score better in tests involving reading when pop music is being played in the background?

Scope and Delimitations

This study focuses on how classical and pop music affects the reading comprehension of Senior High School students. The researchers will select respondents, group them, and will subject them to testing to compare which of the two genres (classical and pop) had the most significant effect on their reading comprehension and how will they differ with one another with respect to this.

This study is delimited to only two (2) genres of music, namely, classical and pop music. The findings are not comprehensive, meaning that this study will not cover all cognitive benefits/disadvantages of classical and pop music, rather, the study’s testing is limited only towards measuring reading comprehension by means of the scores that the respondents will obtain from said testing.
